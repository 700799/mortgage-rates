Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information this website ("we", "us") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the site you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide. If you subscribe to rate alerts, you submit your email address and/or phone number and your notification preferences. These submissions are processed by our form provider (Formspree) and used only to send the updates you requested.
  • Automatically collected data. Like most websites, we and our service providers may collect usage data such as pages viewed, approximate location (from IP), device and browser type, and referring pages, via cookies and similar technologies.

Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and browser storage for two purposes: (1) measuring site usage with analytics, and (2) displaying advertising. Non-essential cookies (analytics and advertising) load only after you accept via our cookie banner. You can change or withdraw your choice at any time by clearing your browser storage for this site, which will show the banner again.

Analytics

When enabled and after you consent, we use Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate, de-identified usage. Google may set cookies to distinguish users. See Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out site-wide with the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

Advertising & affiliate disclosure

Display ads. When enabled and after you consent, we use Google AdSense to serve ads. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies (such as the DoubleClick/DART cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your Google Ads Settings, or opt out of third-party vendor cookies at aboutads.info/choices. For visitors in the EEA/UK, personalized ads and analytics load only with your consent.

Affiliate relationships. Some links on this site — including "Get a quote" buttons and the "Compare today's offers" section — are affiliate or lead-generation links to lending partners. If you click through and request a quote or close a loan, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This never affects the rates a partner offers you, and the rate figures shown elsewhere on the site are independent survey and index data, not partner-sponsored placements.

Email & SMS alerts

If you subscribe, we (and our form processor, Formspree) store the contact details you provide solely to deliver the alerts you opted into. We do not sell your contact information. You can unsubscribe at any time by replying to an alert or contacting us.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under GDPR in the EEA/UK or the CCPA/CPRA in California), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise any right, contact us using the details below.

Data retention

We keep subscription contact details for as long as you remain subscribed. Aggregate analytics data is retained per our analytics provider's defaults.

Children

This site is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
